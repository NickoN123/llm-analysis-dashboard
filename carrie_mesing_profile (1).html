Executive Summary

Carrie Mesing is a global brand equity architect who has elevated Danone's YoPRO into a culturally resonant, athlete-backed performance platform. She combines digital commerce expertise, disciplined brand building, and cross-market orchestration to scale protein-led growth worldwide. Her leadership style is collaborative and highly visible, consistently spotlighting teams and partners while reinforcing a clear "progress" narrative. The next frontier is broadening performance positioning into more expansive everyday wellness territory.

Leadership Highlight

Mesing spearheaded the global activation of Carlos Alcaraz as YoPRO's ambassador and "Chief Progress Officer," executing one of Danone's most coordinated cross-market brand equity launches and strengthening the brand's elite performance credentials.
