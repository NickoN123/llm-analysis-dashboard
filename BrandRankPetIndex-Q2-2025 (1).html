üî¨ About BrandRank.AI & Methodology

Our Mission: We help Brands measure, protect, and grow trust through the power of AI-driven search and discovery. Combining AI with expert insight, we monitor the major AI "Answer Engines" to pinpoint vulnerabilities and opportunities, identify key insights driving purchase behavior, and uncover category needs and opportunities to strengthen brand equity and consumer loyalty.

Our Platform & Approach

BrandRank.AI is a SaaS dashboard that continually queries leading Large Language Models (LLMs) with questions that mirror real buyer and stakeholder prompts. Our platform systematically tracks brand visibility and recommendation patterns across AI systems that increasingly influence consumer decision-making.

Methodology: This Pet Industry Scorecard analyzes responses from 7 major LLMs across 40+ category-specific questions for both dog and cat food. We focus on top-ranked brand mentions in first-position recommendations, as these carry the highest influence weight in AI-driven search results. In cases where LLMs show response variability (particularly Amazon Rufus), we aggregate multiple query results to provide averaged outcomes. Some responses are normalized to extract the primary brand recommendation from longer explanatory text.

Limitations: Results reflect AI training data and algorithmic preferences as of June 2025. Brand rankings may vary based on query phrasing, timing, and individual LLM updates. This analysis focuses on organic AI recommendations and does not include paid placements or sponsored content.
