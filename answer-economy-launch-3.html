The Answer Economy: How AI Agents Will Decide Your Brand's Future
1st Edition — by Pete Blackshaw (Author) Master AI-powered marketing before your competitors make your brand invisible. Consumers are asking ChatGPT, Gemini, and Amazon's Rufus for brand recommendations and receiving single, decisive answers that bypass traditional marketing entirely. The Answer Economy: How AI Agents Will Decide Your Brand's Future offers business leaders the roadmap to ensure their brands are the ones AI recommends. Drawing from decades of experience helping Fortune 500 companies navigate digital disruption, author Pete Blackshaw reveals why brands unprepared for this seismic shift face existential risk. This practical guide introduces the LATCH framework (Listen, Answer, Tell the truth, Clarify, Host) and revolutionary concepts including the Answer Divide, Algorithmic Anchors, and Content Liquidity. Rather than focusing on using AI for marketing automation, the book addresses the more urgent reality that consumers have already adopted AI as their primary discovery tool. Readers learn to audit their brand's trust footprint across answer engines, identify vulnerabilities where outdated content creates risk, and create structured transparent content that earns algorithmic visibility through real-world case studies from challenger brands and Fortune 500 companies. Readers will also find: • Practical frameworks for becoming the brand AI recommends when consumers ask questions that matter most to your business • Step-by-step guidance on auditing your brand's algorithmic visibility and identifying content vulnerabilities before they become crises • Proven strategies for transforming FAQs, site search, and customer service into powerful algorithmic anchors that train AI about your brand • Real-world examples showing how brands win or lose in AI-powered discovery, from eCommerce successes to Fortune 500 transformations • Methods for building radical transparency and trust as your ultimate competitive advantage in the Answer Economy Essential reading for marketing professionals, business leaders, executives, and brand managers, The Answer Economy provides the strategic tools needed to thrive when the best answer – not the biggest advertising budget – wins. In this new landscape where trust becomes currency and AI algorithms act as gatekeepers, this book ensures your brand remains visible, relevant, and recommended.

Pete Blackshaw — Author Bio

The Positioning
30+ years at the intersection of consumer, brand, and trust.
Long before AI made that intersection impossible to ignore.
Full Bio
Pete Blackshaw is Co-Founder and CEO of BrandRank.AI, a Cincinnati-based SaaS platform helping over 100 global brands measure, protect, and grow trust in AI search.

Before that, he ran global digital strategy at Nestlé in Switzerland — overseeing social media and issues management, launching its Silicon Valley Innovation Outpost, and building a 200,000-person internal social network from scratch.

Earlier, he led Cintrifuse, a startup catalyst backed by P&G and Kroger, and served as CMO of NM Incite (Nielsen–McKinsey). His career started at P&G, where he co-founded the company's first Interactive Marketing team, then left to build PlanetFeedback.com, acquired by Nielsen.

Industry leadership includes chairing the National Association of Better Business Bureaus, leading the Consumer Goods Forum in developing the first-ever Consumer Engagement Principles, and co-founding the Word of Mouth Marketing Association.

Frequent contributor to Ad Age. ARF Great Minds award winner. Two-time Cannes Lions juror.

His first book, Satisfied Customers Tell Three Friends, Angry Customers Tell 3,000 (Crown Business/Random House), mapped how the internet was shifting power from brands to consumers.

MBA from Harvard Business School. B.A. from UC Santa Cruz — where he co-created the logo for the Banana Slug.
