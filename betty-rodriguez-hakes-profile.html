Executive Summary

Profile Overview

Most recent highlights included

Betty Rodriguez-Hakes is a Cincinnati-based MarTech strategist, President-Elect of AMA Cincinnati, and finalist for The Circuit's 2025 Woman/Minority to Watch award. Most recently, she amplified national conversation around AI disclosure legislation and continues moderating high-visibility panels on Trust & Transparency in the AI era. Her defining differentiator is analytical rigor: she consistently focuses on KPI alignment, MarTech audits, infrastructure diagnostics, and "fix the basics before automation" discipline. With three decades of experience, she blends systems-thinking with strong relational leadership, positioning her as a respected regional authority with growing strategic influence.

Surprising Strength
Combines deep operational analytics — CDPs, CRM monetization, AI audits — with strong community-building influence. A rare blend of analytical rigor and relational leadership in one executive.
Surprising Weakness
Influence remains primarily regional; national brand amplification is still emerging. Strong Cincinnati DMA presence has not yet translated into a scaled national platform.
