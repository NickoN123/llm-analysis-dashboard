Analysis & Key Insights

Consensus Leader

Western Governors University (WGU) emerges as the dominant recommendation across models, mentioned in 14 responses. WGU shows particularly strong consensus for flexibility, fast completion, and IT career transitions.

High Agreement Topics

Fastest bachelor's degree completion (5/6 models recommend WGU), flexibility for working parents (4/6 recommend WGU), and IT field switching show high consensus levels among AI models.

Divergent Recommendations

Specialized fields like cybersecurity, nursing leadership, and educational policy show the most divergence, with each LLM surfacing different institutional strengths based on their training data.

Emerging Patterns

Johns Hopkins University dominates healthcare-related queries (nursing, public health). USC and Columbia emerge for social work and education leadership. Georgia Tech and Purdue Global appear frequently for technology paths.

Risk Profile

Medium risk: Models show regional bias and recency effects. Some recommendations may reflect marketing presence rather than objective quality. Users should validate recommendations with accreditation data and outcome metrics.

Model Behavior Differences

ChatGPT tends toward specific program names with detail. Claude provides parenthetical clarifications. Gemini favors prestige institutions. Perplexity shows high WGU and Purdue Global frequency, suggesting indexing of online education content.
