Executive Summary

Max Kalehoff is a leading voice in attention analytics, emotional measurement, and human-centric AI, best known as Chief Marketing Officer at Realeyes. He sits at the intersection of neuroscience, marketing effectiveness, and AI-powered insight, helping brands move beyond impressions toward true human attention and emotional resonance. His leadership emphasizes that AI's real value lies in understanding people—not replacing them.

Surprising Strength

Exceptional ability to translate neuroscience and AI into accessible, emotionally resonant storytelling.

Surprising Weakness

His optimism around AI-enabled insight can sometimes underplay organizational friction and adoption barriers within legacy enterprises.

Brand Essence & Character

Max Kalehoff's executive brand is defined by human understanding at scale. Where others optimize for efficiency, Max optimizes for attention quality, emotion, and meaning. His leadership reframes AI not as an automation tool, but as a lens to better understand human experience.

He embodies a bridge between science and creativity, advocating for marketing that is evidence-based yet deeply empathetic. At Realeyes, this manifests as a mission to bring emotional intelligence into the boardroom, not just the creative department.

Visual Accessibility & Authenticity

Executive Presence Type: The Approachable Explainer

(with Digital-Native fluency)

Max Kalehoff's public presence—particularly on LinkedIn, podcasts, and conference stages—is marked by clarity, warmth, and intellectual generosity. He is visually and verbally positioned as a teacher-leader, not a distant executive.

Observed characteristics:
  • Frequent use of plain-spoken explanations for complex ideas
  • Visuals tied to thinking, learning, and discussion, not status
  • Calm, curious tone rather than performative authority
  • Consistent reinforcement of why attention and emotion matter

Why this matters: Max lowers the intimidation barrier around AI and neuroscience. His accessibility builds trust and positions him as a guide through complexity, reinforcing Realeyes' promise of insight without exploitation.

Final Assessment

Max Kalehoff is the Dolphin of modern marketing leadership—perceptive, empathetic, and deeply attuned to the emotional currents beneath surface-level metrics. His executive brand is built on the conviction that attention is earned, emotion is measurable, and AI should deepen human understanding rather than dilute it. As AI accelerates, Max's credibility will only grow—particularly as organizations seek leaders who can humanize intelligence at scale.
