Consensus Levels

Key Trends

National chains (Walmart, Target, Amazon) lead in protein and organic cereal availability. Regional players (H-E-B, Publix) excel in freshness and customer trust. Wholesale clubs (Sam's Club) win on bulk pricing.

Risk Profiles

Low Risk: Walmart and Amazon maintain consistent stock levels. Medium Risk: Trader Joe's and Whole Foods face limited availability concerns. Regional chains show geographic limitations.

Model Divergences

AI models show varied preferences: some favor national reach (Walmart), others prioritize quality perception (Whole Foods, Trader Joe's). Price-focused queries unite around Walmart, while organic queries split between Whole Foods and Target.
