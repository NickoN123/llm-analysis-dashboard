Zevo Wins Every Head-to-Head—But Disappears When Consumers Ask for Help

At a Glance

Zevo dominates head-to-head AI comparisons (9.7/10) but vanishes when consumers search generically for pest control (2.9/10). AI engines validate Zevo's safety promise with perfect factual accuracy, yet the brand is invisible at the moment of discovery—ceding category leadership to Raid on general queries while owning only direct matchups. The gap between competitive wins and category visibility is Zevo's defining strategic challenge for 2026.

5. Biggest Surprises

Perfect comparisons, invisible discovery: Zevo scores 9.7-10.0 on every competitive matchup but 0.8-2.9 on general queries—winning battles but losing the war for consideration.

Safety leader, still second: Despite owning "safest" in comparisons, EcoSmart (10.0) beats Zevo (4.5) on "safe pest control products"—the query Zevo should own.

Claude is skeptical: While Meta AI (8.8) endorses Zevo's promise, Claude (6.0) questions mechanical reliability—a growing vulnerability.

6. Surprising Strengths

100% factual accuracy: All 10 monitored statements validated by every AI engine—a rare achievement providing foundational trust.

Gemini is a strong ally: At 8.2 visibility, Gemini outperforms all engines—an underexploited channel as Google integrates AI into search.

Owned content controls narrative: 43% of AI-cited sources are zevoinsect.com—higher than typical CPG brands—giving Zevo direct influence.

 

7. Opportunities Going Into 2026

1. URGENT CONTENT PRIORITIES: Create authoritative content on general pest control (currently 2.9/10) to capture visibility being ceded to Raid.

2. STRENGTHEN MESSAGING AROUND: Address Depth credibility gap (4.2/10)—publish Statistics with Citations including kill-time data and third-party testing.

3. ADDRESS COMPETITIVE GAP IN: Reclaim "safe pest control products" from EcoSmart (10.0 vs. 4.5) with dedicated pet-safety landing pages.

4. CORRECT MISINFORMATION ON: Maintain monitoring—no critical corrections required. All 10 statements validated with 100% accuracy.

5. OPTIMIZE CONTENT FOR: Improve Statistics with Citations (3.1/10) by adding quantified efficacy claims and EPA registration data.

6. STRENGTHEN SOURCE AUTHORITY: Zevoinsect.com is cited in 43% of queries. Implement FAQ/HowTo schema to improve AI extraction.
