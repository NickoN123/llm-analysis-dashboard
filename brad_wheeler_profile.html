Executive Summary

Brad Wheeler is a systems-driven academic executive who has transformed higher education's digital landscape through strategic innovation, ethical technology leadership, and cross-institutional collaboration. As Indiana University's longtime CIO and current professor at Kelley School of Business, he co-founded game-changing platforms like Unizin and Internet2 NET+, helping public institutions modernize at scale. Recognized in 2024 with both the AIS and INFORMS Impact Awards, Wheeler exemplifies a quiet, powerful brand rooted in collaboration, insight, and execution. While his public presence remains understated, his influence on policy, infrastructure, and shared services is substantial and enduring.

Brand Essence and Character

Brad Wheeler is a visionary systems architect whose work prioritizes open access, institutional collaboration, and ethical technology deployment. His leadership reflects a long-term commitment to durable digital innovation that serves both mission and public value. He is known for building multi-institution ecosystems, such as Unizin and NET+, which have redefined the role of shared platforms in higher education.
