Executive Summary

Robyn Streisand is a seasoned marketing entrepreneur and the Founder & CEO of The Mixx, a strategic creative agency she launched over 25 years ago that now serves major Fortune 500 clients with culturally relevant, inclusive marketing solutions. She also founded Titanium Worldwide, the first collective of certified-diverse agencies designed to elevate diverse supplier participation and drive more equitable outcomes in brand partnerships. Her work centers on embracing community, diversity, equity, and human connection as core drivers of marketing effectiveness.

Surprising Strength

A rare blend of strategic acumen and compassionate leadership, using "lead with love" principles that foster deep client loyalty and team cohesion.

Surprising Weakness

Her emphasis on community and mission can sometimes be perceived as less focused on cutting-edge tech narratives than some younger marketing leaders.

Brand Essence & Character

Robyn Streisand's executive brand is anchored in inclusive transformation—blending cultural relevance with business results. She built The Mixx from her apartment into a thriving agency by prioritizing authentic connection, community insight, and representation long before these became mainstream marketing imperatives. Under her leadership, both The Mixx and Titanium Worldwide have become strategic partners to brands seeking to reflect the complexity and diversity of today's audiences.

Her work champions people-centered storytelling, equitable access to opportunity for diverse suppliers, and DEI as a core business advantage.

Visual Accessibility & Authenticity

Executive Presence Type: The Approachable Connector

Robyn Streisand's public footprint—including interviews, podcasts, and community features—emphasizes human stories, cultural nuance, and shared experience, not just awards or business wins. She is visibly engaged with the LGBTQ+ community, DEI networks, and nonprofit boards, reinforcing her authentic, values-based presence.

Her visual and narrative style centers on:
  • Community engagement over self-promotion
  • Celebration of diversity in practice
  • Relatable storytelling grounded in love and purpose

This combination positions her as a trusted, familiar leader whose visibility strengthens both brand credibility and cultural resonance.
