Align maintains solid positioning as the #1 doctor-recommended probiotic across major AI platforms, with perfect accuracy (100%) and strong promise alignment (7.7/10). The brand's unique Bifidobacterium 35624 strain and clinical backing provide a defensible moat, particularly on conversational engines like ChatGPT, Claude, and Grok.

Methodology: Analysis of 138 category-relevant prompts across 6 AI engines (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, Meta AI, Grok), measuring visibility, accuracy, vulnerability, and content readiness. Data collected October 8-15, 2025.

5. Biggest Surprises

Women's health gap persists: Align's Women's Dual Action line scores 2.2/10 on "best probiotic for hormonal changes"—unchanged from Q4 2025. Garden of Life continues to dominate at 10.0 on the same query. This 8-point gap represents a major missed category opportunity with no improvement quarter-over-quarter.
Perplexity decline accelerating: While Align maintains 8+ scores on ChatGPT, Grok, and Claude, Perplexity visibility has dropped from 4.8 in Q4 to 3.9 now—a 0.9-point decline. Meta AI remains critically low at 3.2. These engines index real-time sources and social content where Align has limited presence.
P&G halo not translating: Despite parent company resources, AI engines do not associate Align with sustainability or corporate responsibility—a missed narrative that Garden of Life (B Corp) actively owns. This gap remains unchanged from Q4.

6. Surprising Strengths

Scientific credibility is a moat: AI engines cite PubMed studies on Bifidobacterium 35624 more frequently than any competitor's clinical evidence—earned authority that cannot be replicated. This positioning remains stable from Q4 2025.

Perfect accuracy maintained: 100% of monitored statements are validated across all engines—no misinformation risk, no correction urgency. Align has maintained this perfect score from Q4 2025, a significant competitive advantage.

Clarity outperforms competitors: Align's 7.8 Clarity score exceeds Garden of Life (6.9) and Florastor (6.9)—consumer-friendly content is a competitive advantage for AI extraction. This strength has remained consistent.

Essential Terms

AI Visibility — "Do I show up?" Measures how often your brand appears when consumers ask AI category-relevant questions. 7+ = AI recommends you; < 4 = invisible.
Brand Vulnerability — "What does AI say about me?" Measures whether AI protects or undermines your reputation across sentiment, performance, value, and experience dimensions.
Brand Promise Alignment — "Does AI believe my story?" Score below 7 means AI hedges rather than endorses. Align's 7.7 indicates strong credibility.
Content Readiness — "Is my site ready for AI?" Can AI find (Availability), trust (Depth), and extract (Clarity) your content? Three-part score reveals specific optimization needs.
Source Influence — "Who shapes my AI narrative?" High owned-source % = greater control over your story. Align's 27% citation rate is solid but can improve.
Statements of Fact — "Is AI telling the truth?" Monitors accuracy of specific brand claims across engines. Align's 100% accuracy is a significant competitive advantage.
Engine Breakdown — AI engines (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, Meta AI, Grok) use different training data and algorithms, so brand perception varies. Understanding engine-specific performance reveals strategic priorities.
