Executive Summary

Most Recent Highlight

Jill Cress has demonstrated remarkable agility in aligning the legacy tax giant H&R Block with the expectations of a new generation. She introduced AI-driven tools like Tax Assist, celebrated cultural moments in real time, and built deep brand equity through storytelling and innovation. Her personal side hustle, the launch of Hetta Gløgg, a Scandinavian mulled wine brand, amplifies her authenticity as a human-centric leader.

Surprising Strength

Her ability to use culture as a marketing tool, tapping into zeitgeist moments to make H&R Block relevant beyond tax season.

Surprising Weakness

Her wide-ranging focus on tech innovation, CX, brand, small business, and side ventures occasionally diffuses a single, coherent executive narrative.
