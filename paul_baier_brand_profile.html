Executive Summary

Paul Baier's work as CEO of GAI Insights continues to shape enterprise AI adoption strategies, with his frameworks including the widely used WINS and Own Your Own Intelligence (OYOI) published and referenced in leading industry outlets and academic settings.

Surprising Strength

Paul consistently bridges frontier AI strategy with executable corporate playbooks, making him one of the most pragmatic voices in enterprise generative AI.

Surprising Weakness

His strong focus on enterprise and strategy can sometimes obscure his broader ecosystem contributions outside executive circles, including community outreach and workforce development.

Brand Essence & Character

Paul Baier exemplifies practical strategic leadership in generative AI adoption. Rather than abstract futurism, he emphasizes real business outcomes, measurable ROI, and risk-aware transformation. As leader of GAI Insights, he promotes clarity, context, and actionability in a landscape often clouded by hype.
