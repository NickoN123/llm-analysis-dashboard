No public narrative on climate, sustainability, or DEI. In an era where stakeholders expect visible corporate responsibility, this absence could become reputational liability.

Non-PR Native Background

Strong in media and data but still establishing credibility in Edelman's core domains of reputation management and crisis communications. May face scrutiny from traditional PR practitioners.

May face cultural friction integrating Edelman's global structure. Silo resistance possible as platform-first approach challenges traditional agency model and regional autonomy.
