Methodology & Measurement Framework

AI Visibility (Hero Score): Measures frequency with which Vans is recommended as top choice across relevant category queries. Evaluated across 6 major AI platforms (OpenAI, Gemini, Anthropic, Perplexity, Meta AI, Grok) using 80+ consumer queries spanning product categories, brand comparisons, and purchase intent.

Brand Vulnerability: Assesses brand health across 10 dimensions (Authenticity, Safety, Promise Clarity, Brand Sentiment, Product Performance, Transparency, Price-to-Value, Sustainability, Customer Experience, Innovation) based on AI system analysis of available information.

Content Readiness: Evaluates technical infrastructure for AI discovery across three dimensions: Clarity (ease of understanding), Availability (structured data and schema), Depth (technical specifications, citations, expert validation).

Promise Alignment: Measures how consistently AI platforms communicate brand promise when discussing the brand. Based on explicit evaluation of brand promise delivery across all six platforms.

Accuracy: Tests factual correctness of brand-specific claims across AI platforms, identifying misinformation, misattribution, or contradictions.
