Executive Summary

Henry Innis is a visionary marketing analytics and measurement leader, driving Mutinex's mission to transform how marketers make investment decisions through data-driven insights and foundational modeling. Under his leadership, Mutinex has achieved rapid growth, expanded internationally (including strategic US expansion), and won industry leadership recognition—most recently being highlighted for pushing back against legacy marketing mix measurement approaches.

Recent Highlight:

Mutinex, under Henry Innis's leadership, recently made waves at the Marketers & Money Conference, distinguishing itself from tech-giant MMM partnerships, reinforcing its position as an independent and transparent analytics innovator.

Surprising Strength

Radical candor about leadership flaws and mental stress, which builds deeper authenticity and trust.

Surprising Weakness

Despite tech and product strength, scaling people systems and delegation remain challenges he publicly acknowledges.

Brand Essence & Character

Henry champions a data-first, decision-oriented product vision, blending analytics excellence with actionable business tools. His approach to leadership is deeply strategic, balancing bold vision with pragmatic product development. He consistently frames Mutinex not as a reporting tool, but as a "growth engine" for marketers, emphasizing transparency, usability, and high-performance outcomes.
