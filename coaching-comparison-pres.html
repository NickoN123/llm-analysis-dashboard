Executive Summary

Three Archetypes of Excellence

Elephant

Bill Self

A towering symbol of legacy and power, deeply entrenched in Kansas history. The Elephant represents institutional memory, unwavering standards, and the ability to carry the weight of tradition while delivering championships.

Fox

Jon Scheyer

Agile, calculated, and working to shape a dynasty that is both familiar and future-ready. The Fox represents strategic intelligence, adaptability, and the clever navigation of building a new era while honoring the past.

Falcon

Rick Carlisle

Sharp, evolutionary, and always ready to adapt altitude and approach to win. The Falcon represents high-perspective strategic vision, swift decision-making, and the ability to empower others while maintaining precision.
